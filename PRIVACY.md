PRIVACY POLICY – The Link Digital Security Scam Verification Engine

Last updated: 2025-02-06

The Link Digital Security ("we", "us", "our") operates the Scam Verification Engine, a free public tool designed to help users identify potential scams, fraud, phishing, vishing, and impersonation attempts.

We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), Act 4 of 2013.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data only:

Automatically collected (non-identifying):

IP address (anonymized)

Device and browser type

Scan metadata (type of scan, timestamp)

User-provided (optional):

Email address (only if you opt in)

Scam reports submitted voluntarily

We do not collect:

Identity numbers

Banking credentials

Passwords

OTPs

2. Purpose of Collection

Your data is used to:

Provide scam risk analysis

Improve scam detection accuracy

Maintain community scam statistics

Send safety alerts or educational updates (email opt-in only)

3. Email Capture & Consent

Emails are collected only with explicit consent and used for:

Scam alerts

Security education

Product updates

You may unsubscribe at any time.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We may share aggregated, anonymized statistics with:

Law enforcement

Banking partners

Cybersecurity researchers

5. Data Retention

Scan metadata: up to 12 months

Email records: until opt-out

Community reports: anonymized indefinitely

6. Your Rights (POPIA)

You may:

Request access to your data

Request correction or deletion

Withdraw consent

Contact: admin@checkascam.co.za

7. Security Measures

We implement:

Encryption at rest and in transit

Rate limiting

Abuse detection

Secure API key management

8. Waitlist & Digital Footprint Scanner

When you join our waitlist for the Digital Footprint Scanner, we collect:

- Your email address (required)
- Your preferred tier (R29 or R59)
- The source page where you signed up

We use this information to:

- Notify you when the scanner launches
- Send you a launch discount code
- Understand demand for each tier

**Data retention**: We keep your email until you unsubscribe or the scanner is launched (whichever is later). You can request deletion at any time by emailing admin@checkascam.co.za.

**Your rights**: You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data under POPIA. We do not share your email with third parties.

9. Disclaimer

This tool provides risk indicators only and does not replace professional, banking, or law enforcement advice.
